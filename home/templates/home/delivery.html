Delivery

Our standard method of delivery is flat packed and box. As these can vary so much in size and weight, depending on your chosen dimensions, we have a tiered scale for delivery charges. You will be informed of the delivery cost for your chosen dimensions along with the cost of the unit it self.

The delivery tiers are as follows:

Tier 1: £10.00
Tier 2: £20.00
Tier 3: £30.00
Tier 4: £40.00

Smaller units are sent as standard parcels while larger ones are handled by our trusted freight courir.

We can also arrange delivery of pre-assembled units. Please contact us if you wish to discuss this further.

Please allow 2-3 weeks for dispatch of your unit and a further 5 days for transit. If there is a further daley for some reason we will notify you.
