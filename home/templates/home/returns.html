Returns

We are concerned that you are 100% satisfied with your unit and if you are not it can be returned to us within 28 days for a full refund.

If the reason for return is not a fault with the cupboard we ask that you are responsible for return postage costs. We also ask that the cupboard is packaged similarly for return as it was received.

Minor losses of fixings are overlooked but damage to your cupboard that was not incurred in transit may result in the cost not being refunded in full.

All cupboards come with a 5 year guarantee for normal use. This excludes any damage incurred if relocating your unit, attempting to alter it or any other willful or reckless damage.
